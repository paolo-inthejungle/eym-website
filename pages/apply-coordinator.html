Apply as National Coordinator

Fill in the form below to apply as a national coordinator for the European Youth Movement in your country.

What does a National Coordinator do? National Coordinators are EYM's local representatives. They recruit members, organise activities in their country, and act as a bridge between their national community and the EYM Executive Board.
If your country already has a coordinator, we may invite you to assist them.

Questions? Write to us at info@eym-europe.eu
